# 🎯 5️⃣ UX Design Principles for This Product

We design for:

1. **Fast booking (under 60 seconds)**
2. Mobile-first (70–80% of users)
3. Large tap-friendly time slots
4. Clear pricing
5. Zero confusion about availability
6. Clean aesthetic (beauty clinic feel)
